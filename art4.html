Fascism. Nobody is happy with the state of the country, left or right. Everyone seems disillusioned with the ideas of American exceptionalism and individualism so they retreat into a fantasy of the West, where individuals still prevail, where this country seemed like it still had purpose. It is an escape to picture oneself in a world where you can just pack up and move to the frontier to start again amidst a reality where most are static, locked into the same few career paths and cultures. Where this escape becomes problematic is when it becomes a tool of propaganda. Under the third reich, Germans were promised a future that honors their past of a unified, traditional German people and the existence ancient aryan civilization. All this despite the real history of Germany being barbarism, mud huts, and warring states. Propaganda under Mussolini often painted a romanticized and idealized image of ancient Rome to bring more into the fold of fascism. The vast majority of western media upholds these contradictions, spouting revisionist history that paints natives as a step away from beasts with a whitewashed cast. These bits of western media enforce the idea that settlers where right to take what was free, their deaths at the hands of natives was because of anti white of racism and not counter terrorism, and that the less white neighbors to the south were uneducated scoundrels that would do anything to swindle you out of your cash or saddle. And now, ICE agents wear cowboy hats and the Department of Homeland Security posts paintings of pioneers in wagons with the caption "Remember your Homeland's Heritage".
